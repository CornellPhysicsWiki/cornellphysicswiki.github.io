# Atomic, Molecular & Optical (AMO) Physics
Atomic, Molecular, and Optical (AMO) Physics is the study of matter-matter and light-matter interactions at the atomic and molecular level, typically dealing with one or a few atoms/molecules. 
It focuses on understanding the structure of atoms, molecules, and their interactions with electromagnetic fields.
